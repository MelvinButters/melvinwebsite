Fizzlebang’s Radiant Punch Enhancement Protocol

Scientific-ish Methodology for Increasing Holy Kinetic Output — Revision IV

Recipient: Paladin Designate, bearer of excessively shiny convictions

Before beginning, remember: if your fist starts glowing before step 4, stop immediately and unclench. Last time you lit the curtains on holy fire.

I. Purpose of This Instruction

This guide details proper invocation and biomechanical alignment for channeling controlled radiant force through a physical strike (“punch”). The goal is amplification of divine kinetic transfer without vaporizing your own knuckles or spontaneously sanctifying your gloves.

II. Pre-Strike Preparation (Don’t Skip These!)

  • Sacred Focus Regulation: Align internal divine resonance to 1.2 lumen-cycles. If your hand tingles too early, reduce to 0.9.
  • Intent Clarification: Mentally assert one approved smiting statement (e.g., “Begone, evil!” or “Stop hitting my friends!”) while maintaining controlled breathing.
  • Stance Stability Nodes: Feet must be planted at a 37° outward angle to avoid sideways holiness leakage. Learn from beta tester Greg.

III. Component Notes (Paladin-Friendly)

1. Gauntlet or Bare Fist Conduit

Your striking surface must be free of grime, corruption, or mysterious goo of uncertain origin. Holy energy dislikes sticky textures and may redirect into your elbow.

2. Radiant Vector Focus

A small symbolic token (sunburst, feather, tiny glowing rock you found and decided was holy). Must be on your person. Doesn’t have to be used dramatically, but it helps.

IV. The Math-y Bits You Probably Won’t Read

Radiant output (“Holy Punch Power” or HPP) is estimated using:

HPP = (Φ * sin(α)) / √|γ + iρ|

Where:

  • Φ = Divine charge density (in smite-joules)
  • α = Angle of your punch arc
  • γ = Ambient holiness coefficient
  • ρ = Number of moral grievances currently on your mind

If the result includes an unexpected imaginary component, drink water and think more wholesome thoughts.

V. Radiant Punch Execution Sequence

  1. Draw divine energy to your fist by clenching lightly and inhaling with intent (not too hard — we discussed this).
  2. Channel faith along your arm like a warm current. If it feels fizzy, that’s normal. If it feels crunchy, stop.
  3. Wind up your punch at a controlled pace of 1 holy syllable per heartbeat. Recommended mantra: "Lux… Solis…".
  4. Release punch with exhalation, ensuring radiant energy exits no earlier than 0.04 seconds before knuckle impact.
  5. Aim with purpose. Radiance is deeply judgmental and may veer toward nearby sinners.

Troubleshooting

  • Punch glows the wrong colour: Check alignment. Green glow indicates nature magic. Purple indicates accidental necromancy. Fix immediately.
  • Fist feels "lighter than air": You overcharged. Touch the ground. Preferably dirt.
  • Target explodes more than expected: This is technically a success, but note it for calibration.
