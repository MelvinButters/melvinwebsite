Fizzlebang’s Radiant Punch Enhancement Protocol — Apprentice Notes

Instructions Left Behind for the Next Poor Soul Learning the Art

Recipient: Apprentice, do not set fire to yourself

Before beginning: if your fist starts glowing before the designated step, stop immediately and unclench. We have rules about curtains and walls.

I. Purpose of This Instruction

This guide shows you how to safely channel radiant force into a punch. The goal is a controlled holy strike without self-inflicted fireworks. Follow it precisely; these are my old notes for you to survive your training.

II. Pre-Strike Preparation (Do Not Skip)

  • Sacred Focus: Align internal energy to a calm level. If your hand tingles too soon, lower your focus and breathe.
  • Intent: Pick a simple smiting phrase and repeat mentally while breathing steadily.
  • Stance: Feet at a 37° outward angle to avoid sideways holiness leakage. Learn from my mistakes.

III. Components You Must Have

1. Striking Surface

Whether gauntlet or bare fist, it must be clean and free of mysterious goo. Sticky hands redirect energy into your elbow.

2. Radiant Token

A small symbolic item (sunburst, feather, or glowing rock). Carry it with you — it helps the energy stay on track.

IV. Apprentice-Friendly Punch Power

Forget scary math. Think of your Holy Punch Power as:

Holy Punch Power ≈ Divine Energy × Punch Angle ÷ Stability Factor
  • Divine Energy = How focused and righteous your fist is
  • Punch Angle = The smooth path your fist travels
  • Stability Factor = Calmness + control over distractions

If your punch fizzles, wobbles, or glows weird colors, take a deep breath, adjust your stance, and try again.

V. Punch Execution Sequence

  1. Draw divine energy by lightly clenching and inhaling with intent.
  2. Channel faith along your arm like a warm current. Fizzy = good; crunchy = stop.
  3. Wind up at one holy syllable per heartbeat. Suggested mantra: "Lux… Solis…".
  4. Release your punch with exhalation, ensuring radiant energy exits just before impact.
  5. Aim carefully. Radiance judges intent and may veer toward nearby sinners.

Troubleshooting

  • Glow is the wrong colour: Green = nature interference; Purple = necromantic cross-signal; Red = your anger.
  • Fist feels lighter than air: You overcharged. Touch the ground. Preferably dirt.
  • Target explodes too much: This means you listened correctly. Note for calibration next time.
