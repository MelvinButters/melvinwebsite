Kenku Hot Chocolate Recipe
  • Heat 1 cup of milk until it gently steams — "bubble-bubble!"
  • Mix 2 tbsp cocoa, 1 tbsp honey, pinch of cinnamon & tiny salt into a paste.
  • Whisk hot milk into the paste — "scritch-scritch" good luck stir.
  • Reheat briefly to warm it fully.
  • Top with whipped cream and toasted seeds — tap the cup twice: "tak-tak!"
Ingredients
  • 1 cup milk (or oat milk)
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Pinch of cinnamon
  • Tiny pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Toasted seeds or crushed nuts
Kenku Notes
  • Toasted seeds are traditional — crunchy and warm.
  • Kenkus believe stirring clockwise adds mimicry clarity.
  • Best served in clay cups warmed by a lantern flame.
Fun Extras
  • Add a drop of vanilla for “soft-night” sweetness.
  • Use maple syrup instead of honey for forest-style brew.
  • A sprinkle of nutmeg makes it tavern-ready.
